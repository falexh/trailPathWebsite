How do you know which trail fits you?



  • Determine your strengths. How far are you willing to trek?
  • Determine your motivation. Are you adventuring for leisure, or for discovery?
  • Know your experience. Trust the system and don't be overconfident!
  • Understand your environment. Be aware at all times.
  • Always come prepared! Never forget water!
  • Hike with a partner!
